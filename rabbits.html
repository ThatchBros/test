Super Smash Bros

Super Smash Bros. is a series of crossover fighting video games published by Nintendo, featuring characters from various Nintendo franchises.

Gameplay

Super Smash Bros. games are primarily multiplayer games where the objective is to knock opponents off the stage to score points.

Characters

The games feature a large roster of characters, including famous Nintendo characters such as Mario, Link, Pikachu, and others.

Stages

Each game features various stages inspired by Nintendo franchises, providing diverse environments for battles.

Items

Items are often dropped into the stages during battles, providing temporary power-ups or weapons to players.
